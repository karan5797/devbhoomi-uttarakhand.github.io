SOME FACTS :



            

            The meaning of "Dev Bhoomi" in English is "The Lnd of God". The title of  Dev Bhoomi is given to
            Uttarakhand by Rishi and  Muni’s. Uttarakhand is very famous with the name Dev Bhoomi. There are
            many reasons behind this. Uttarakhand is famous with the name Dev Bhoomi from ancient times.

            Rishi and Muni’s called this place Dev Bhoomi. There are many reasons why Uttarakhand is called
            Dev Bhoomi.

            Some of the reasons are given below :-

            1. According to legends. There are 5 Khand or parts of Dev Bhoomi. These parts are
            Kashyapa(Kashmir), Kangra, Kedar, Kurma, Kailash.  Two parts of Dev Bhoomi came under
            Uttarakhand . These parts are Kedar and Kurma. Kedar Khand or part is ruled by Garhwal Kingdom
            and Kurma Khand or part is also called Kumaon or Manaskhand and ruled by Kumaon Kingdom.

            2. Uttarakhand is called Dev Bhoomi because Uttarakhand is the place where people meditate on God
            and become the part of God. Uttarakhand is the place where people can do meditation without any
            distraction and disturbance.

            3. Legends say that many Devi’s and Devta’s live in these region. 

            4. Because of Many Religious places like Haridwar, Chhota Char Dham, Char Dham, and many other
            temples and religious places. The worship places of Lord Shiva and Vishnu are here.  Panch Badri,
            Panch Kedar, Panch Prayag pilgrimages are in Uttarakhand. Uttarakhand have many tirth sthala.

            5. If you will go to a place where someone meditated or worshiped, you will also get benefited by his
            act. Because many rishi muni’s stayed here and they meditated and worshiped here. People came
            here and get benefited. The travel of People not only from India but from all over the world is called
            Yatra in Sanskrit language. People come to Uttarakhand for Yatra.

            6. Sidha and Sadhu’s come to Uttarakhand for meditation. 

            Uttarakhand is one of the places in India which was almost saved from every invasion.  That is why 
            Uttarakhand is one of the most peaceful place in India.
